About
CANDELA
JALIL

I'm a graphic designer based in Sydney, Australia with a focus on visual identity, editorial design, and digital experiences. I approach every project as a visual argument — a deliberate point of view on how form and function can coexist beautifully.

My work spans brand systems, packaging, web design, and typographic experimentation. I'm drawn to projects where the visual language carries the weight of the message — where design doesn't just communicate, it resonates.
