A BRAND BUILT
FOR Motion

Paquex is a logistics brand operating at the speed of now. The challenge: create a visual identity that communicates precision, reliability, and energy — without falling into the generic language of the industry.

The solution: a system built around motion. Every element — from the logotype to the brand applications — carries a sense of direction, speed, and arrival.
