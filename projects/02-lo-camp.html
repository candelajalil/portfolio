About the Project

Designing about what exceeds

Lo Camp is an editorial book exploring the eponymous aesthetic: a system of sensibility that privileges artifice over nature, exaggeration over restraint, theatricality over seriousness. The book translates this philosophy into every design decision — typography, colour, composition and structure.

The central challenge was this: how to design a book about excess without falling into chaos? The answer was to build a rigorous grid system and then deliberately break it at key moments. Order as the foundation of elegant disorder.
