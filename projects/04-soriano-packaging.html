Without Erasure

Soriano Biscuits had decades of shelf presence and consumer recognition. The task was to modernize without abandoning — to make the brand feel current without making loyal customers feel lost.

The rebrand introduced a cleaner hierarchy, a more refined color palette, and improved legibility — while maintaining the essential brand equity built over years on Argentine supermarket shelves.
