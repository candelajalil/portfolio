The Challenge
01

THE
Problem

Green Eat's existing app was visually cluttered, structurally confusing, and failed to communicate the brand's core promise — fresh, honest, convenient food. Users dropped off at the menu stage due to a lack of visual hierarchy and trust signals.

The Solution
02

THE
Answer

A research-driven redesign built around speed, clarity, and appetite. Every screen decision was made to reduce friction, increase trust, and put the food — not the interface — at the center of the experience.
