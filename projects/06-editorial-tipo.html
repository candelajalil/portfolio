This editorial piece explores what happens when typography stops being a vehicle and becomes the destination. Letters as architecture. Letterforms as landscape. White space as silence.

The publication experiments with scale, rhythm, and tension — pushing type until it breaks its own rules, then finding what remains when the rules are gone.

"When the word dissolves, the letter remains. When the letter dissolves, the shape remains. What remains after the shape — that is the design."
