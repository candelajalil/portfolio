Context
"Space is the brand made physical — every material choice is a brand decision."

Soriano needed to establish a strong physical presence at Expo Retail Saludable — one of Argentina's leading health food trade events. The challenge was to translate a visual identity into three-dimensional space without losing its warmth or commercial clarity.

The stand had to work at multiple scales simultaneously: readable from 20 meters, engaging up close, and memorable in photographs.
