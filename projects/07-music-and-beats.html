Music & Beats is an electronic music festival that needed a visual identity as energetic and uncompromising as the music itself. The challenge was to create a brand that reads in both digital darkness and festival daylight.

The identity is built around contrast — electric lime against deep black. Typography as loud as the bassline. A system that works from a 3×3cm ticket barcode to a 10-meter festival banner.
