Concept

THE ARRESTED
Moment

Oakley is not a brand that asks you to slow down. It builds equipment for people who push past the limit. The poster had to capture that energy in a single frozen frame — the instant before impact, the breath before the drop.

The direction: maximum visual tension within a static medium. Composition as force. Typography as velocity. Every element in service of the arrested moment.

Process
01
DIRECTION

Defining the visual concept: tension, speed, arrested motion. Reference gathering from sport photography, film stills, and Oakley's brand archive.

02
COMPOSITION

Structural exploration — placement of figure, product, and type. Testing diagonal tension against the format. Finding the hierarchy that creates kinetic energy in a still image.

03
REFINEMENT

Typography as velocity. Color grading as mood. Final balance between the brand's bold language and the raw energy of the subject.
