Design Decisions
01
TERROIR
PALETTE

Warm earth tones derived from Argentine landscape — terracotta, wheat, olive and aged leather. A palette that evokes provenance before a single word is read.

02
FOOD-FIRST
HIERARCHY

Photography leads. Every layout decision subordinates text to image. The product is the hero — the interface steps aside to let it speak.

03
EDITORIAL
TRUST

The typographic system communicates authority without coldness. Serif headlines carry warmth; mono body text signals precision. A balance between artisan and professional.
